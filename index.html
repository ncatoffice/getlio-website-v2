How it works

Three Steps. One Less Thing to Worry About.

Lio doesn’t read your life—it identifies your tasks. Our local AI scans only for bills and renewals, keeping your personal conversations strictly private and on your device.

01

Secure Connection

Connect your Gmail in seconds.

Lio uses Google’s most secure OAuth standard. We request read-only access—meaning Lio can identify a bill, but it can’t send an email to your boss or delete a message from your mom. You stay in total control of the “keys” to your inbox.

02

Private, Local Analysis

Zero-Cloud Architecture

Your data never leaves your phone.

Lio uses your phone’s built-in AI. We never store your emails on our servers, so your private information is never at risk of a cloud data breach. Your data stays under your physical control.

03

The Pulse: Your Life, Filtered

Stop hunting. Start acting.

Lio ignores the newsletters, the spam, and the social alerts. It surfaces only the items that actually need your attention today:

“Your car insurance renews in 2 days—it’s $12 higher than last month.”
“The warranty on your dishwasher expires Friday. File that claim now.”
“Comcast bill is due tomorrow. Tap to pay.”

Lio securely connects to your Gmail account using Google OAuth to read only billing, renewal, and warranty-related emails. All email classification happens on your device—your inbox data is never sent to external servers for routine processing.

Features

What Lio does for you

The Pulse

Open the app, see what needs doing. Your 1–3 most urgent items—a bill due Friday, a renewal charging next week, a warranty about to expire—ranked by deadline, not by when the email arrived.

The Vault

Every appliance, subscription, warranty, and insurance policy you own—in one place. Lio populates it from your email. Ask “when does my Dyson warranty expire?” and get an answer.

Anomaly Detection

Your electric bill jumped from $120 to $185? Lio flags it before autopay drains the difference. You decide whether to pay, dispute, or investigate.

Warranty Tracking

Lio extracts warranty details from your purchase receipts. When your MacBook Pro or Dyson Airwrap coverage is about to expire, you hear about it weeks early—not after it’s too late.

Smart Notifications

No spam. No marketing noise. Lio only notifies you about bills approaching their due date, renewals about to charge, and warranties about to expire. That’s it.

Delete Everything

The Nuke Button

One tap. Every piece of data Lio has ever stored—classification results, vault entries, sync history, OAuth tokens—deleted instantly. This also revokes Lio’s access to your Gmail at Google’s servers. Not “within 30 days.” Not “after review.” Immediately. No backups survive.

You trusted Lio with access. The Nuke Button is how you take it back, completely, whenever you want.

We built this because we’re users too. If we wouldn’t want a company holding our data forever, we don’t think you should have to either.

Data Safety

Your Data, Your Control

Lio is built on a simple principle: your email is none of our business. We just help you manage it. You decide how much Lio sees—from 100% manual control to fully automated on-device assistance, you’re always the one holding the keys.

We do not sell your data to third parties

Zero data brokers. Zero ad networks. Zero analytics SDKs. Your data stays on your device and nowhere else.

We cannot use your data for advertising

Lio is built without an advertising engine. Because your email processing happens entirely on your device, your data is never accessible to us or any third-party ad network. Your inbox content can never be used to target, profile, or market to you—it’s technically impossible by design.

No human can read your email

All processing is automated and happens exclusively on your device. We have built Lio so that we cannot see your email content. No human at Lio will ever have access to your inbox unless you choose to share specific data to help us resolve a support request.

On-device AI processing

Lio’s classification pipeline runs entirely on your iPhone using Apple’s on-device models. Emails never leave your phone for routine operation.

Restricted Read-Only Access

Lio cannot send, delete, modify, or take any action on your Gmail account. Our integration is strictly limited to identifying billing, renewal, and warranty data. You remain the sole owner of your inbox, and you can revoke access at any time through your Google Security settings.

Zero Tracking. Zero Fingerprinting.

Lio will never ask for permission to track you. We don’t use tracking SDKs, we don’t follow you across other apps, and we don’t phone home with your usage habits. Your life admin stays between you and your device.
